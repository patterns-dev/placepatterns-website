Wicking Bed

A wicking bed is a high production growing system that uses a relatively small amount of water. It is ideal for environments where there is limited water supply.

The defining element of a wicking bed is an underground resevoir of water. The soil layer, above the resvoir, wicks the water up to feed the plants above...
